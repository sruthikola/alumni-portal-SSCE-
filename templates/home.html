About Our Alumni Portal

The Alumni Portal is designed to connect graduates of Sri Sivani College of Engineering, fostering lifelong relationships and creating opportunities for professional growth.

Our mission is to build a strong, supportive community where alumni can network, share opportunities, mentor current students, and stay connected with their alma mater.

Whether you're looking for career opportunities, wanting to give back, or just stay in touch with classmates, our portal provides the tools you need.
